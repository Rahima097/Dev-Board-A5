Question-2: What is the difference between innerHTML, innerText, and textContent ?

The innerHTML, innerText, and textContent are used to manipulate the content of elements in the DOM. They might seem similar bus important differences in their behavior and use case. Here some short differences
innerHTML: Gets or sets the HTML content of an element including tag.
innerText: Gets or sets visible text content, considering CSS.
textContent: Gets or sets the text content ignoring HTML tags and CSS.including hidden text.
